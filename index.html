How to Play Relay Race

Begin by rolling the dice at the bottom right corner of the screen, by clicking it. Based on the number of dots on the die, you must either draw a card containing a question or roll the dice again. A point is earned each time a question is answered correctly. If the question is answered incorrectly, the computer wins a point. Remember, you must outscore the computer to win the game.
